About Color X

Founded in 2006 as Golden Printing & Signages, we later rebranded as COLORX Advertising and have since evolved into one of the leading providers of printing and signage solutions in the United Arab Emirates. Over the past 19 years, we have built a reputation for setting high standards in the industry, consistently delivering quality products at competitive prices with a commitment to fast turnarounds. Our experienced team, combined with our dedication to excellence, has helped us earn the trust of over 500+ satisfied customers

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to become the leading advertising and branding company in the GCC and beyond. We aim to achieve this by delivering innovative, high-quality services while maintaining the best possible value for our clients.

Building and sustaining momentum for your business starts with making your presence known to the world. At Colorx Advertising, we combine creativity, cutting-edge technology, and a deep understanding of culture to create connected brands that not only drive value for our clients but also make a lasting impact globally.
