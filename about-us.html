About ColorXAD

Founded in 2006 as Golden Printing & Signages, we later rebranded as COLORX Advertising and have since evolved into one of the leading providers of printing and signage solutions in the United Arab Emirates. Over the past 19 years, we have built a reputation for setting high standards in the industry, consistently delivering quality products at competitive prices with a commitment to fast turnarounds. Our experienced team, combined with our dedication to excellence, has helped us earn the trust of over 500+ satisfied customers
